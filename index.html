Keep your creativity flowing with Leap

Leap is Kontakt’s fun, performance-ready sampler that captures ideas as they happen. Load loops, one-shots, or kits and start reshaping them in real time. It combines the spontaneity of a looper with the freedom of sampling, so you can experiment and turn impromptu moments into tracks without slowing down.
